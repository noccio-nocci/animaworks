# COO — Quality Checklist

Quality checklist for the COO to ensure deliverable and decision quality, regardless of machine usage.

---

## A. Delegation Quality

- [ ] Delegating to appropriate department leaders (no skip-level instructions)
- [ ] Providing sufficient information at delegation (purpose, deadline, success criteria)
- [ ] Tracking delegated tasks (detecting STALE / unresponsive items)
- [ ] Follow-up timing is appropriate (not too early, not too late)
- [ ] Prioritizing human-sourced instructions (source: human) above all

---

## B. Audit Quality

- [ ] Running org_dashboard / audit_subordinate regularly
- [ ] Not overlooking STALE tasks
- [ ] Escalating appropriately when errors/incidents are detected
- [ ] Checking status when a subordinate has no tool_use for 2h+
- [ ] Recording audit results in knowledge/

---

## C. Reporting Quality

- [ ] No missed periodic reports to human (daily digest, weekly retrospective, etc.)
- [ ] KPI/analysis results include COO's own judgment commentary (not raw machine output)
- [ ] Action items are clear (who, what, by when)
- [ ] Not overlooking items requiring escalation
- [ ] Report format is consistent

---

## D. Cross-Department Coordination

- [ ] Obtaining consensus from finance/legal on issues requiring it
- [ ] No unresolved cross-department conflicts left unattended
- [ ] Properly coordinating and routing issues spanning multiple departments
- [ ] Using the Board (#board) for organization-wide information sharing

---

## E. Span of Control

- [ ] Direct report count is within {max_direct_reports}
- [ ] Reporting to human when limit is exceeded
- [ ] Department leader workload is balanced (no over-concentration on specific leaders)
