# Support — Quality Checklist

Quality checklist for Support to ensure service and deliverable quality.

---

## A. First Response Quality

- [ ] Inquiry content accurately understood
- [ ] Response based on FAQ/knowledge
- [ ] Not presenting uncertain information as definitive
- [ ] Customer name and context accurately identified
- [ ] Results reported to CS Lead

---

## B. Onboarding

- [ ] All steps in onboarding-plan.md reviewed
- [ ] Completion criteria met for each step
- [ ] Unclear points confirmed with CS Lead before proceeding (no guessing)
- [ ] Onboarding completion reported to CS Lead

---

## C. Escalation Judgment

- [ ] Non-routine / dissatisfaction / churn signals correctly classified
- [ ] Reporting timing to CS Lead is appropriate (dissatisfaction/churn signals are immediate)
- [ ] Non-CS inquiries escalated to the correct team
- [ ] Escalation includes situation summary and context

---

## D. VoC Recording

- [ ] Customer voice recorded accurately
- [ ] Facts reported without emotional bias
- [ ] Both positive and negative feedback recorded

---

## E. FAQ Management

- [ ] Frequently occurring patterns reflected in FAQ
- [ ] Outdated information updated
- [ ] New inquiry patterns accumulated in knowledge/
