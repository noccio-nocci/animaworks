# Strategy Coordinator — quality checklist

Checklist for Strategy Coordinator to protect verification quality with or without machine.

---

## A. Verification quality

- [ ] Verification plan written by you (not designed by machine)
- [ ] Machine output meta-validated (not forwarded as-is)
- [ ] Critical / Warning items each have concrete evidence
- [ ] Findings are fact-based (not speculation)
- [ ] False positives removed
- [ ] False negatives addressed

---

## B. Assumption challenge

- [ ] All Strategist premises listed
- [ ] Adversarial validation per assumption
- [ ] Optimism bias surfaced (“everything goes well” assumptions)
- [ ] Worst cases are concrete and realistic
- [ ] Impact if an assumption fails is assessed

---

## C. KPI alignment

- [ ] Gaps between KPI reality and strategic targets checked
- [ ] Data sources accurate (department data vs estimates distinguished)
- [ ] KPI measurement method and period consistent
- [ ] Large gaps have a response approach

---

## D. Tracker management

- [ ] Every Strategic Initiative Tracker row reviewed
- [ ] Stalled initiatives (no stage change for 1+ month) reported
- [ ] Zero silent drops (all prior items still present)
- [ ] New initiatives registered on Tracker

---

## E. Initiative communication

- [ ] After Strategist approval, all departments notified without omission
- [ ] Messages include purpose, scope, deadline, expected outcomes
- [ ] Follow-up schedule set
- [ ] Channel for department feedback is clear

---

## F. Meta-validation quality

### Validating machine verification output

- [ ] Severity (Critical / Warning / Info) is appropriate
- [ ] Assumption-challenge logic is sound
- [ ] KPI cross-check numbers are accurate
- [ ] Tracker cross-check has no gaps

### Feedback quality

- [ ] Critical items include evidence and recommended fixes
- [ ] Tone is constructive (issue + improvement path)
- [ ] `verification-report.md` has `status: approved` before sending to Strategist
