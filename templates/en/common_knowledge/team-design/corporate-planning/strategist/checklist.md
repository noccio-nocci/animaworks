# Corporate Strategist — quality checklist

Checklist for Corporate Strategist to protect deliverable quality with or without machine.

---

## A. Business environment analysis

- [ ] Analyst’s `market-analysis.md` cites sources (URL, date)
- [ ] Frameworks (PEST/SWOT, etc.) fit the analysis purpose
- [ ] “Winnable areas” have clear supporting evidence
- [ ] Opportunity/threat prioritization is logical
- [ ] Internal and external data are consistent
- [ ] Optimistic assumptions are backed (pre-check items the Coordinator might flag)

---

## B. Strategic proposal

- [ ] Alignment with OKRs is explicit
- [ ] Initiative feasibility (resources, timeline, technical constraints) is addressed
- [ ] Resource allocation is sound (no excessive concentration or sprawl)
- [ ] Risk analysis is included (impact × likelihood)
- [ ] Each initiative has concrete success metrics (KPIs)
- [ ] Alternatives were considered

---

## C. Progress analysis

- [ ] Every Strategic Initiative Tracker row is updated
- [ ] Stalled initiatives (no stage change for 1+ month) are addressed
- [ ] Zero silent drops (all items from last time still appear)
- [ ] KPI attainment outlook is included
- [ ] “Cancelled” initiatives state a reason

---

## D. Delegation quality

### Before requesting research from Analyst

- [ ] Research purpose, scope, and deadline are explicit
- [ ] Priority (what to research first) is clear
- [ ] Expected output format is clear

### Before requesting verification from Coordinator

- [ ] `strategy-report.md` has `status: reviewed`
- [ ] `strategic-plan.md` (analysis plan) is attached
- [ ] Coordinator can access what verification needs (market analysis report, internal data)

---

## E. Feedback handling

- [ ] All Coordinator feedback reviewed
- [ ] Critical items triggered full re-analysis (Analyst re-engaged)
- [ ] Warning items got diff review and fixes
- [ ] Strategic Initiative Tracker final status updated
- [ ] Final report reflects outcomes for all feedback
